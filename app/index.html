Get Answers to All Your Questions

FAQ #1

Does Move Matcher Move my stuff?
No. Move Matcher works with our roster of pre-screened movers to collect their best bids to move your belongings. We provide you each mover's best bid as your quote.

FAQ #2

What kind of moving companies do you represent?
All of the companies on our roster are thoroughly vetted, and are licensed and insured. Our proprietary rating system uses reviews from previous customers as well as Yelp and Google scores to eliminate sub-par moving companies.

FAQ #3

Will I get a barrage of calls from movers?
No. We don't share your personal information with any moving companies. When you're ready to select a moving company's bid, you contact them and work out the details.
